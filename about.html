About the Market

Discover the endless resources that exist within the community. Rooted in the idea that we each have something to offer and we each have needs that arise, the Needs and Offers Market is a unique solution to close the gap.

Whether your need is a 3-night stay near Downtown, fragrance-free laundry detergent, or a tour of Seattle Center, this Market connects you with a neighbor who can help get that need off of your checklist.

The Needs and Offers Market was inspired by the many needs-and-offers events that occur in town, and developed by four software developers during their time at a Seattle developer academy.

How it Works

Have a need? Visit the Home page, submit a sticky note, and make your needs known! Your need will be posted to the Need Listings, along with the other submitted needs for all community members to view. To fill out a need, simply enter your name, best contact email, and a short description of your need, then submit.

Have an offer? This Market is a two-way street. If there is a community need you can meet, feel free to jump in and make an offer. Drag and drop each sticky note that you can fulfill into the summary section below. At the end, you will be able to send a separate email to each person notifying them of your offer.
